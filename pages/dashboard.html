Pachmarhi Hill Staion

Often called the "Queen of Satpura," is a hill station in Madhya Pradesh, known for its scenic beauty and spiritual significance.

It is home to the Jata Shankar and Mahadeo Temples, which are dedicated to Lord Shiva and are nestled amidst caves and lush forests.

Kanha National Park

A vast expanse of grassland and forest in the central Indian state of Madhya Pradesh. Tigers and other animal's can be spotted in Kanha Meadows.

The elevated plateau of Bamhnidadar is home to birds of prey.The park’s flora and fauna are documented in the park's Kanha Museum.
